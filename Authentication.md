| **Privileged Identity Management (PIM)**       | ❌ No                              | ❌ No                             | ✅ Yes                            |
| **Dynamic Groups**                             | ❌ No                              | ✅ Yes                            | ✅ Yes                            |
| **Access Reviews**                             | ❌ No                              | ❌ No                             | ✅ Yes                            |
| **Entitlement Management**                     | ❌ No                              | ❌ No                             | ✅ Yes                            |
