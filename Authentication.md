| **Hybrid Identity (Sync with on-prem AD)**     | ✅ Yes                             | ✅ Yes                            | ✅ Yes                            | ✅ Yes                        |

| **Dynamic Groups**                             | ❌ No                              | ✅ Yes                            | ✅ Yes                            | ✅ Yes                        |
